About Us

We are your specialist for chauffeured ground transportation, based in Switzerland and servicing Europe wide. Our professionalism has enabled us to offer consistent Top-Class Services and exceeding the high expecations of our clients. Since 2001 we've been proudly offering Limousine services at the World Economic ForumA nnual Meeting in Davos.
Chauffeur Drive is a brand of the amag Group and Member of the Swiss Limousine Association and the National Limousine Accociation as well as Offical Partner of Switzerland Tourism.
